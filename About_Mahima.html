About Mahima Ministries

Mahima Ministries is dedicated to transforming lives through compassionate service and community empowerment. Our mission is to provide support, resources, and opportunities to those in need, fostering hope and creating lasting change.

Our History

Founded in 2005 by Sarah and David Patel, Mahima Ministries began as a small initiative to address the immediate needs of underprivileged communities in Mumbai. Driven by a deep faith and a commitment to social justice, the Patels started by providing food and shelter to homeless individuals and families. Over the years, their efforts expanded to include educational programs, healthcare services, and vocational training, reaching thousands of individuals and families across India.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals and communities to overcome poverty and injustice through holistic development programs. We strive to create a society where everyone has access to basic necessities, education, and opportunities to thrive. We are committed to upholding the dignity and worth of every person, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our Values

At Mahima Ministries, our work is guided by the following core values: * **Compassion:** We approach every interaction with empathy and understanding, recognizing the inherent worth of each individual. * **Integrity:** We are committed to transparency, accountability, and ethical conduct in all our operations. * **Service:** We believe in selfless service and putting the needs of others first. * **Empowerment:** We aim to equip individuals and communities with the skills and resources they need to become self-sufficient. * **Collaboration:** We work in partnership with local communities, organizations, and volunteers to maximize our impact.
