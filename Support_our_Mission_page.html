Support Our Mission

Your support helps us provide essential services and create lasting change in the lives of children and families. Explore the various ways you can contribute to our mission.

Donate

Make a one-time or recurring donation to support our programs. Your contribution, no matter the size, makes a significant impact.

Sponsor a Child's Expenses

Sponsor a child's daily expenses, including food, clothing, and shelter, to provide them with a stable and nurturing environment.

Sponsor the Education of a Child

Support a child's educational journey by sponsoring their school fees, books, and other educational materials, ensuring they have access to quality education.

Help the Abandoned Elderly and Destitute

Provide care and support to abandoned elderly individuals and destitute people by contributing to their daily needs, medical care, and shelter.

Help with the Health Expenses of Our Children and Elderly

Assist in covering the medical expenses of children and elderly individuals under our care, ensuring they receive necessary healthcare and treatments.

Volunteer

Join our team of volunteers and contribute your time and skills to support our programs. We offer various opportunities to get involved.

Partnerships

Partner with us to create a greater impact. We welcome collaborations with organizations and individuals who share our vision.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please contact us at support@mahimaministries.org or call us at (555) 123-4567.
