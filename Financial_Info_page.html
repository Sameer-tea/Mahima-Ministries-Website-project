How Donations Are Used

Your donations directly support our programs and initiatives. Here's a breakdown of how we allocate funds:

Program Support

60% of donations go directly to supporting our various programs, including education, healthcare, and community development.

Operational Costs

20% of funds are used to cover essential operational costs, such as facility maintenance and utilities.

Outreach and Events

10% of donations are allocated to outreach activities, events, and awareness campaigns.

Administrative Expenses

10% of funds are used for administrative expenses, ensuring efficient management and compliance.

We are committed to using your donations effectively and responsibly to maximize our impact. Thank you for your support!
