Child Care Institution

Our Child Care Institution provides a nurturing home for orphaned and abandoned children, offering education, healthcare, and emotional support. We strive to create a family-like environment where children can thrive and reach their full potential.

Old Age Home

Our Old Age Home offers a safe and caring environment for elderly individuals, providing them with comfortable living spaces, nutritious meals, and medical care. We aim to ensure their well-being and dignity in their golden years.

Impact Stories

Upper Primary School

Our Upper Primary School provides quality education to children from underprivileged backgrounds, equipping them with the knowledge and skills necessary for a brighter future. We focus on holistic development, including academics, sports, and extracurricular activities.

HIV/AIDS Patients Care

Our HIV/AIDS Patients Care program provides comprehensive support to individuals affected by HIV/AIDS, including medical care, counseling, and social support. We aim to improve their quality of life and reduce the stigma associated with the disease.
